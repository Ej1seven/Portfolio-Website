Project Overview

Pig Game is a game I created consisting of two players in competition to reach a hundred points. How the game works is one player rolls the dice and as long as the number they roll is not a one that number is added to their current score. The player has the option to roll again or hold. If the player holds then their current score is added to their total score. If the player rolls again they have the possibility to increase their current score, but they run the risk of rolling one and setting their current score to zero.
